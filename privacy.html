Privacy Policy

This page explains what data Half Awake Eyes collects through the website, why it is collected, and how to get in touch if you have any questions.

Updated May 2026

What We Collect

Half Awake Eyes operates this website and uses Firebase, a Google service, to collect limited analytics data such as page views, link clicks, video interactions, ticket redirects, and smart-link activity. This helps us understand general engagement with our music, shows, and content.

The data stored may include a timestamp, page path, anonymous session ID, viewport size, referrer, campaign or UTM details, and non-personal interaction details such as the link or button used.

Mailing List Signups

If you join the mailing list, we collect the email address you submit. We may also store basic signup context, such as the page you signed up from, campaign details, referrer, and signup date, so we can understand where signups came from and manage the list.

We use mailing list data only for Half Awake Eyes updates and related admin. You can ask for your email address to be removed at any time using the contact details below.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No names through public analytics logging
  • No email addresses unless you submit one through a mailing list form
  • No sign-up accounts for public visitors
  • No sale of visitor data to third parties

Cookies and Browser Storage

The site uses session storage in your browser to keep an anonymous session ID, avoid duplicate page-view logs, and limit repeated analytics events during a visit. This storage is temporary and is normally cleared when your browser session ends.

The site does not set its own advertising cookies by default. Some ticket or smart-link pages may use Meta Pixel tracking if a campaign has been configured with a Pixel ID.

Why We Process This Data

We rely on our legitimate interest in understanding how visitors interact with our website, music, videos, shows, and promotional materials. For mailing list signups, we process the email address you provide so we can send band updates and manage the list.

Third-Party Processing

Firebase may process technical information, such as IP addresses, as part of providing its infrastructure and security services. That processing is handled by Google under its own service terms and data processing arrangements.

Where Meta Pixel is enabled for a ticket or smart-link campaign, Meta may receive event information such as page views or outbound ticket clicks. This is used only for campaign measurement connected to that page.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, want to request deletion of mailing list data, or want to request deletion of logged data associated with a campaign or identifier, contact halfawakeeyes@gmail.com.
