Privacy Policy

This page explains what limited analytics data Half Awake Eyes collects through the website, why it is collected, and how to get in touch if you have any questions.

What We Collect

Half Awake Eyes operates this website and uses Firebase, a Google service, to collect limited analytics data such as page views, link clicks, video interactions, and redirects. This helps us understand general engagement with our music and content.

The data stored may include a timestamp, page path, non-personal link or interaction details, and a random or campaign ID if one is supplied in a URL.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No names or contact details through analytics logging
  • No sign-up accounts for public visitors
  • No advertising profile data
  • No sale of visitor data to third parties

Why We Process This Data

We rely on our legitimate interest in understanding how visitors interact with our website, music, videos, and promotional materials. The purpose is internal insight only, such as seeing what content gets attention and which links are being used.

Third-Party Processing

Firebase may process technical information, such as IP addresses, as part of providing its infrastructure and security services. That processing is handled by Google under its own service terms and data processing arrangements.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or want to request deletion of any logged data associated with a campaign or identifier, contact halfawakeeyes@gmail.com.
