About the Band

Half Awake Eyes is a four-piece progressive metal band from Glasgow known for their blend of crushing riffs, intricate bass work, and dynamic vocals. Their music sits between the atmospheric and the aggressive — heavy enough to move you, textured enough to hold you there.

Influenced by modern progressive and post-metal, the band prioritises feel, space, and storytelling over constant intensity. Their sound is immersive and deliberate, offering a raw yet focused experience both live and in the studio.

Built on a strong collaborative foundation and a DIY mindset, Half Awake Eyes is currently recording their debut EP and building momentum following their first live appearance — ready to bring their distinct sound to more stages across Scotland and beyond.
